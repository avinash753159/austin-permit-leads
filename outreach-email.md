# Cold Outreach Templates

## WHO TO EMAIL
Subcontractors and material suppliers in Austin who would want to know about new projects BEFORE they hit the bid boards.

Target trades:
- Electrical contractors
- Plumbing contractors
- HVAC contractors
- Drywall/framing
- Concrete/foundation
- Roofing
- Painting
- Flooring
- Window/door suppliers
- Lumber/material suppliers

## WHERE TO FIND THEM
1. Texas TDLR license database (public): https://www.tdlr.texas.gov/verify-a-license/
2. Google Maps: search "[trade] contractor Austin TX"
3. Yelp/Thumbtack business listings
4. ABC (Associated Builders and Contractors) Central Texas chapter directory
5. Austin permit data itself — the GCs pulling permits are your leads too
